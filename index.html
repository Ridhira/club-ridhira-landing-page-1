Co-ownership at Club Ridhira allows you to own a share of a luxury property, giving you access to the property for 4 weeks per year, along with shared responsibility for its maintenance and management costs.

Unlike timeshares, which only give you the right to use a property, co-ownership at Club Ridhira gives you a deeded share in the property, making you a partial owner with equity in the property.

Club Ridhira Co-ownership offers the luxury of owning a high-end property at a fraction of the cost, allowing for potential property appreciation. Additionally, shared costs for maintenance and management make it a more cost-effective way to enjoy luxury property ownership.
